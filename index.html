Biography

Ja Morant, born on August 10, 1999, is an American professional basketball player who plays for the Memphis Grizzlies in the NBA. Known for his explosive athleticism and exceptional playmaking skills, Morant has quickly become one of the most exciting young players in the league.

Morant grew up in Dalzell, South Carolina, and played college basketball at Murray State. His standout performances there led him to be the second overall pick in the 2019 NBA Draft.

Career Highlights

  • NBA Rookie of the Year (2020)
  • All-Star selection (2022)
  • NBA All-Rookie First Team (2020)
  • NBA All-Star Game MVP (2023)
