Biography

Ja Morant is an American professional basketball player for the Memphis Grizzlies of the National Basketball Association (NBA). Born on August 10, 1999, in Dalzell, South Carolina, Morant rose to prominence during his college career at Murray State University.

Career Highlights

  • Drafted 2nd overall in the 2019 NBA Draft by the Memphis Grizzlies.
  • NBA Rookie of the Year 2019-2020.
  • Named to multiple NBA All-Star Games.
